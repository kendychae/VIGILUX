# VIGILUX Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 27, 2026_

VIGILUX ("we", "our", "the App") is a community incident reporting application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By creating an account or using the App you agree to the practices described here.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the App.

**You provide directly:**

- Account info — name, email address, password (stored as a salted hash), and optional phone number.
- Profile info — display name and optional avatar.
- Report content — incident description, category, severity, and any photos you choose to attach.

**Collected automatically when you use the App:**

- Precise device location, only at the moments you create or view a report on the map.
- Device and diagnostic data — app version, OS version, crash logs, and basic performance metrics.
- Push notification token, if you enable notifications.

**We do NOT collect:**

- Continuous background location.
- Contacts, calendar, microphone audio, or health data.
- Payment information (the App is free and contains no in-app purchases).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
- To attach accurate location and timestamps to reports you create.
- To route reports to the appropriate public-safety officers.
- To send push notifications about status changes on your reports.
- To investigate abuse, prevent fraud, and protect users.
- To improve App stability and performance.

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do **not** use it for advertising or third-party tracking.

## 3. How We Share Information

- **Officers and administrators** see reports relevant to their assignments, including the reporter's display name and the report's location and content.
- **Service providers** that we use to operate the App — hosting (Render), database (PostgreSQL), and push delivery (Apple Push Notification service / Firebase Cloud Messaging) — process data only on our behalf under contract.
- **Legal requests** — we may disclose information if required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of users.

## 4. Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active.
- Reports are retained for as long as needed for the agency's records, after which they may be archived or deleted.
- You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time (see Section 7).

## 5. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards:

- TLS 1.2+ for all network traffic.
- Bcrypt password hashing.
- Short-lived JWT access tokens with refresh-token rotation.
- Role-based access control on every API endpoint.
- Rate limiting and OWASP Top-10 hardening.

No system is perfectly secure. If we discover a breach affecting your data we will notify you as required by applicable law.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You may at any time:

- View and edit your profile in the App.
- Revoke location, camera, photo, or notification permissions in your device settings.
- Request a copy of your data, or request deletion, by emailing **privacy@vigilux.app**.
- Sign out from any device on the Profile screen.

Depending on where you live (e.g., the EU/UK under GDPR or California under CCPA/CPRA) you may have additional rights to access, correct, port, or restrict processing of your personal information. We will honor those requests where applicable law requires.

## 8. International Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you use the App from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed there.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted in-app and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
